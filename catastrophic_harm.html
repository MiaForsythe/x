Nuclear Weapons History History of the TPNW

Knowledge of nuclear weapons technologies will allow us to find ways to prevent threats to the real world, as well as to escape from possible attacks, save people's lives. The purpose of this course project is to educate in the field of nuclear safety, taking into account the principles of operation of nuclear weapons, the consequences of such practices and the further impact on both people and their subsequent lifestyle, and on their environment. By understanding the nature of the processes of formation of nuclear explosions themselves, their effects on all living things and tracing patterns in the change of matter, identifying factors and prerequisites affecting the development of this industry as a whole, fixing mistakes and major turning points in history during the period of formation and study of current topics, mastering basic survival techniques, one can come to comprehension the task at hand. The target audience consists of both ordinary people, of all segments of the population, and specialists who need technical reinforcement of knowledge in this area. The information will be of a general educational nature and will not give more accurate data in the accuracy of the design.
